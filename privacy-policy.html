Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Habit Tracker Mobile Application developed by Uttam Wakchaure.

1. Information We Collect

  • Profile Information: Display name, email address, and profile picture when you create an account.
  • Authentication Data: We use Firebase Authentication (email and password login).
  • Habit Data: Habits you create, completion history, and streaks.

  • Advertising Data: Google AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Service.

  • To sync data across devices.
  • To enable social friend connections.
  • To improve the application.
  • To display advertisements via Google AdMob.

3. Data Storage and Security

We use Firebase (a Google service) to store and secure user data. Security rules and encryption are implemented to protect information.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. However, third-party services such as Firebase and Google AdMob may process data according to their privacy policies.

5. Your Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data from the app settings at any time.
